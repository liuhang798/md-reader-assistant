# Design QA

This document records the final visual and interaction verification for the current release.

## Verified surfaces

- Home, reader, split editor, About dialog, and update dialog.
- Simplified Chinese and English interfaces.
- Light and dark themes.
- Table-of-contents navigation, recent-file removal, search, print, and back-to-top.
- Editor focus, Markdown highlighting, save/save-as, and unsaved-change protection.
- Transparent application icon at 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128, 192, and 256 pixels.
